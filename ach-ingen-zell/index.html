-ach, -ingen, -zell

A visual exploration of the spatial patterns in the endings of German town and village names.

This especially shows the most common locations of German villages ending on -ingen and on -ing.

This is a fork of Moritz Stefaner's project (http://github.com/moritzstefaner/ach-ingen-zell). Please have a look at his site to see a lot more suffix mappings.

Moritz Stefaner, 2016
